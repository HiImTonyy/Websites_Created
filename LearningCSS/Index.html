FF14 Hardcore Ideas

  1. Not allowed to use Aetheryte's (fast travel), even in cities unless required to by a quest (teleporting back to the entrance WHILE inside a dungeon is ok).
  2. Cannot use the Market Board.
  3. Joining a FC is ok but once you join a FC, you can't ever join a different one even if you get kicked out or leave. the exception is if it gets disbanded.
  4. Cannot receive any sort of item(s) from another player unless it is someone in your FC that has been in the FC for 10+ days (must be given in person).
  5. Cannot use the FC chest.
  6. Using both land and flying mounts is ok.
  7. No limits to how many times you can go through a dungeon.
  8. In order to repair your items, you must repair them YOURSELF.
  9. You can sell your items with someone in your FC (who has been in there for 10+ days) or with a complete stranger. you MUST do it in person and not through mail.
    10. 10.1 The stranger must be as the name suggests, a stranger. it cannot be a person you spoke with outside the game.
    10.2 Harassment is not allowed. when they say no thanks to what you want to sell, move on.
  10. Using linkshells is ok.
  11. When you die, you are dead for good and must start a new character. no revives of any-kind.

Warhammer 40K Intro

It is the 41st Millennium. For more than a hundred centuries The Emperor has sat immobile on the Golden Throne of Earth. He is the Master of Mankind by the will of the gods, and master of a million worlds by the might of his inexhaustible armies. He is a rotting carcass writhing invisibly with power from the Dark Age of Technology. He is the Carrion Lord of the Imperium for whom a thousand souls are sacrificed every day, so that he may never truly die. Yet even in his deathless state, the Emperor continues his eternal vigilance. Mighty battlefleets cross the daemon-infested miasma of the Warp, the only route between distant stars, their way lit by the Astronomican, the psychic manifestation of the Emperor's will. Vast armies give battle in his name on uncounted worlds. Greatest amongst his soldiers are the Adeptus Astartes, the Space Marines, bio-engineered super-warriors. Their comrades in arms are legion: the Imperial Guard and countless planetary defence forces, the ever vigilant Inquisition and the tech-priests of the Adeptus Mechanicus to name only a few. But for all their multitudes, they are barely enough to hold off the ever-present threat from aliens, heretics, mutants - and worse.

To be a man in such times is to be one amongst untold billions. It is to live in the cruelest and most bloody regime imaginable. These are the tales of those times. Forget the power of technology and science, for so much has been forgotten, never to be re-learned. Forget the promise of progress and understanding, for in the grim dark future there is only war. There is no peace amongst the stars, only an eternity of carnage and slaughter, and the laughter of thirsting gods. But the universe is a big place and, whatever happens, you will not be missed.
