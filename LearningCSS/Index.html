FF14 Hardcore Ideas

  1. Not allowed to use Aetheryte's (fast travel), even in cities unless required to by a quest (teleporting back to the entrance WHILE inside a dungeon is ok).
  2. Cannot use the Market Board.
  3. Joining a FC is ok BUT once you join FC, you can't ever join a different one even if you get kicked out or leave. the exception is if it gets disbanded.
  4. Cannot receive any sort of item(s) from another player unless it is someone in your FC that has been in the FC for 10+ days (must be given in person).
  5. Cannot use the FC chest.
  6. Using both land and flying mounts is ok.
  7. No limits to how many times you can go through a dungeon.
  8. In order to repair your items, you must repair them YOURSELF.
  9. can sell your items with someone in your FC (who has been in there for 10+ days) or with a complete stranger. you MUST do it in person and not through mail.
    10. 10.1 The stranger must be as the name suggests, a stranger. it cannot be a person you spoke with outside the game.
    10.2 Harassment is not allowed. when they say no thanks to what you want to sell, move on.
  10. Using linkshells is ok.
  11. When you die, you are dead for good and must start a new character. no revives of any-kind.
