Book Description

In "The Golden Compass," Lyra's this 11-year-old on a mythic quest. She's clueless about her real deal and heads from East Anglia to the Arctic, hunting for her snatched buddy Roger and her locked-up uncle, Lord Asriel. It's a wild, icy adventure packed with twists, turns, and epic discoveries!

Author: Philip Pullman

Number of Pages: 432
