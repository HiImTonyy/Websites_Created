Book Description

In "American Psycho," Bateman's this slick, good-looking Wall Street guy by day, but at night, he's a total nightmare. He's all about living out his darkest fantasies through some seriously twisted torture and murder. Bateman's like the walking, talking end-of-the-world kind of horror that's too wild for society to even handle.

Author: Bret Ellis

Number of Pages: 416
