Book Description

In "Fight Club" by Chuck Palahniuk, you've got this unnamed dude battling hardcore insomnia. When his doc brushes off his sleepless woes, he starts crashing support groups, pretending to be super sick. It's all about finding some weird kind of peace in a life that's off the rails. A total mind-bender of a book, man!

Author: Chuck Palahniuk

Number of Pages: 224
