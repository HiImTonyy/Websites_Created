Book Description

In "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone," 11-year-old Harry finds out he's a wizard and heads to Hogwarts. Soon, he's caught up in a gnarly quest to stop a wicked wizard from snagging the Sorcerer's Stone, which would make him super powerful and immortal. It's a magical, spellbinding adventure, dude!

Author: J.K Rowling

Number of Pages: 320
