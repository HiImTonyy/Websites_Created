Book Description

The Alienist" by Caleb Carr is this rad, mind-bending thriller set in 1896. It's got Dr. Lazlow Kreizler, a cool alienist (that's old-school for psychologist), who teams up with four others to get inside the head of a mystery psycho offing kid hustlers in NYC. It's a dark, twisty page-turner, taking you deep into the gritty streets and the even grittier minds.

Author: Caleb Carr

Number of Pages: 512
