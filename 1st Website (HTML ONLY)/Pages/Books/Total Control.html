Book Description

In "Total Control," Sidney Archer's world flips when her hubby vanishes in a wild plane crash. She's left sifting through a maze of secrets and lies. With a killer job, a cool husband, and a rad daughter, Sidney's life seemed perfect. Now, she's on a high-stakes hunt for the truth in this twisty, page-turning thriller.

Author: David Baldacci

Number of Pages: 672
