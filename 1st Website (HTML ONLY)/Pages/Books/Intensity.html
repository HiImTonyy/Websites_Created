Book Description

In "Intensity," there's this unhinged psycho, Edgler Foreman Vess, breaking into a house to wipe out everyone inside. Calling himself a “homicidal adventurer,” Vess is all about chasing wild thrills, no fear, no regrets, living life on the edge. It's a hardcore, adrenaline-pumped nightmare!

Author: Dean Koontz

Number of Pages: 343
