Game Description

Dragon Quest IV is this epic JRPG where you level up, throw down with wicked monsters, and cruise through an awesome storyline.
It's all about grinding, getting stronger, and diving deep into a killer tale. Total blast for any gamer into the RPG scene!

Some Weapons

Metal Babble Sword

The MBS is packin' a massive attack of 135, making it a beast of a weapon that'll straight-up shred your enemies!
Dragon Killer

The Dragon Killer is the big-ticket item in the game, setting you back a hefty 15,000 gold coins. It's packing a 95 in the attack department, which, okay, might not sound like top-of-the-line, but c'mon, that name alone is wicked enough to give your enemies the jitters. It's like the ultimate "don't mess with me" label!
Sword of Miracles

The Sword of Miracles is this killer blade that's all about that 100 attack power. It's like your go-to gear for those miracle plays you need to take down the gnarly monsters out there. Trust, with this sword in hand, you're gonna be laying down some serious hero vibes!
