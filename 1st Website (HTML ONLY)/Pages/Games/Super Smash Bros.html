Game Description

You're throwin' down as classic Nintendo characters in all-out, no-holds-barred brawls.
Choose from legends like Mario, Link, and Pikachu, and battle it out in wild, dynamic arenas.
Knock your opponents off the stage with crazy moves and power-ups.
Whether you're solo or smashing it up with friends, it's a non-stop, action-packed throwdown where only the raddest fighters come out on top!

Some Items

Poke Ball

The Poke Balls are like your secret weapon stash. Toss one, and out pops a Pokémon ready to back you up with their special moves. Each one's got a different trick to help the player who threw it, making battles even more unpredictable and totally awesome!
Beam Sword

The Beam Sword is where it's at for reach and power. This thing glows a wickedly and packs a serious punch. Unlike other items, it doesn't run out – and chucking it as a projectile? That's where it really shines, delivering some majorly powerful blows. It's like the ultimate combo of style and strength in a sword!
Hammer

The Hammer is a total game-changer. Snag one, and your character goes ham, swinging like a maniac. And if you land a hit? Whoever's on the receiving end goes flying like they're shot out of a cannon. It's one of those ultra-powerful items that can totally turn the tide of a battle!
