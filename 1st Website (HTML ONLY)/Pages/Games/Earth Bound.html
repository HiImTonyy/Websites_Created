Game Description

You're Ness, a rad kid with psychic powers, teaming up with a crew of cool friends. You're on this wild quest to stop an evil alien, Giygas.
Travel through quirky towns, battle out-there enemies, and unravel trippy mysteries with your PSI powers.
It's a totally tubular RPG adventure with some far-out humor and funky beats!
