Game Description

It's this epic tale all about these four orphaned kids who get pulled into this wild adventure by a crystal of light.
This rad crystal hooks them up with some of its own power and is like, "Yo, you gotta jet and bring the world back into harmony."
It's all about their journey to make things right. Total quest of destiny vibes!

Weapons

Air Knife
Yo, the Air Knife is this slick weapon that's rockin' a solid 60 in the attack department, making it one of the top-tier weapons for your thief. And check this – it ain't just about slicing and dicing. When you use it as an item, it busts out Aero, blowing away your enemies like they're caught in a windstorm. No joke, it's like having a weather forecast of pure domination!
The Onion Sword
The Onion Sword is the ultimate beast of a blade, slammin' down a massive 156 Attack and bumpin' up all your stats by +5. But catch this – only the Onion Knight's got the chops to wield it. And talk about rare – this bad boy's only dropped by Green, Red, and Yellow dragons. But man, when you snag it, it's like hitting the jackpot. Totally worth the grind!
Elder Staff
This weapon is like one of the most wicked staffs you can snag for your mage. And here's the kicker: when you whip this thing out as an item, it casts Cura! That's right, it's not just a beatdown stick, it's also got some serious healing mojo. Perfect for keeping your squad in the game!
