Game Description

Super Mario Bros 3 is this bangin' platformer where you cruise through levels snaggin' power-ups, squashing gnarly goombas,
and your prime mission is to rescue Princess Peach from that wicked turtle dude, Bowser!

Power Ups

Super Mushroom

These mushrooms totally morph regular Mario into Super Mario. When he's all super-sized, he can bust bricks with mad style. But watch out, 'cause if Super Mario takes a hit, he's gonna bounce back to classic Mario mode.
Star

The Star makes Mario like, totally untouchable for a hot minute, lettin' him blast through baddies just by charging 'em. But keep it real, 'cause he ain't all-out bulletproof – my man can still wipe out if he takes a dive into a no-way-out pit, gets crispy in lava, clocks out of time, or gets chomped in one of those gnarly auto-scrolling levels.
Tanooki Suit

So when Mario rocks the Tanooki Suit, he flips into Tanooki Mario, ya dig? This getup lets you pull off all the dope moves Raccoon Mario can, like soaring high, spinning to whack foes, and bustin' bricks. But check this – with the Tanooki threads, Mario can also go full-on zen and morph into a statue. Totally rad for dodging baddies or just chilling in style.
