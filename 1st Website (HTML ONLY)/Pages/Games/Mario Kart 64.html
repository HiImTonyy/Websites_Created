Game Description

You're racing as your favorite Mushroom Kingdom characters, tearing up wild tracks full of twists, turns, and wacky power-ups.
It's all about speed, dodging bananas, and nailing your rivals with red shells.
Whether you're battling it out in multiplayer mayhem or going for gold in Grand Prix, it's a full-throttle, laugh-out-loud racing riot!

Extra Mode
