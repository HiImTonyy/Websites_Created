Game Description

You're stepping into a brutal battle arena, choosing from a lineup of rad warriors with killer moves.
It's all about fast-paced, bone-crunching fights, mastering insane combos, and finishing off your opponent with those outrageous Fatalities.
Whether you're going solo or facing off against your buds, it's a gnarly, action-packed throwdown!
