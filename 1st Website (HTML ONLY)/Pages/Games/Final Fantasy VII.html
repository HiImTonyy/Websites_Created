Game Description

You step into the boots of Cloud Strife, teaming up with rebels to battle the mega-corp Shinra and the ominous Sephiroth.
It's a wild mix of intense battles, exploring a large world, and mastering a combo of tech and magic.
Your mission? Save the planet from total destruction while unraveling Cloud's complex past. It's an epic quest packed with action and deep storylines.

Some Weapons

Ultimate Weapon

Cloud's Ultimate Weapon is the real deal. It's got these sky-high stats, plus eight linked Materia slots for maxing out your magic and skills. And here's the kicker – this bad boy deals more damage the higher Cloud's HP is. So, the tougher Cloud rolls, the harder this weapon hits. It's like Cloud's powerhouse move, turning him into a major-league buster on the battlefield.
Princess Guard

The Princess Guard is Aeris's ultimate weapon. This powerhouse comes with seven Materia slots, six linked, boosting Aeris's stats big time, both in physical and magical defense. But here's the twist – the Princess Guard's damage doubles if one party member is KO'd, and triples if two are down. It's like Aeris becomes a major force when the going gets tough, turning setbacks into serious smackdown power!
Premium Heart

Tifa's Premium Heart is a total knockout. This weapon's stats are off the charts, with eight linked Materia slots for maxing out your magic and skills. But the real game-changer is its unique mechanic: the damage it deals is tied to how full Tifa's Limit gauge is. The more charged up the gauge, the harder she hits. With Premium Heart, Tifa can unleash massive damage, especially if you play your cards right and keep that Limit gauge pumped. It's all about timing and strategy, making Tifa a powerhouse in battle.
