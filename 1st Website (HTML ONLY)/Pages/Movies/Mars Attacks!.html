Movie Synopsis

In "Mars Attacks!," Martian spaceships park it over Earth's big cities, and everyone's eyeballing 'em, wondering if they're chill or not. The U.S. Prez, James Dale, gets the word from Professor Kessler that these Martians are supposedly here to play nice. But when a meet-and-greet fries Congress, the military dudes are all about hitting back with nukes. It's a wacky, wild intergalactic showdown!
