Movie Synopsis

In "Starship Troopers," Earth's future is all about battling these humongous alien bugs dead set on wiping out humans. Not much is known about these Bugs, but they're bad news. Before all this war craziness, there were chill times. Now, the Mobile Infantry's jetting off to alien planets, taking the fight to the Bugs. These aliens are hardcore, with one goal: their survival, no matter what. It's a wild, interstellar throwdown!
