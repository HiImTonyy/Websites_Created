Movie Synopsis

In "Candyman," Helen digs into this spooky urban legend about a dude with a knife-hand named Candyman, linked to some creepy local murder. Then this mysterious guy, just like the legend, starts tailing her. Helen's getting freaked, thinking this Candyman myth might be way more than just a scary story. It's all eerie vibes and chilling suspense!
