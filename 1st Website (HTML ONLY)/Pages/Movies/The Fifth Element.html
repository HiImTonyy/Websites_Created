Movie Synopsis

In "The Fifth Element," cabbie Korben Dallas from future NYC gets the shock of his life when Leeloo, the key to saving the world, drops into his cab. She's the fifth element and has to team up with the other four to stop some mega Evil thing from wrecking Earth. Dallas, a space priest, and the wild Ruby Rhod gotta hustle to outdo the bad guy Zorg and save the day. It's a sci-fi rollercoaster with all the futuristic bells and whistles!
