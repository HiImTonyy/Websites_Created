Movie Synopsis

In "Event Horizon," a space crew, with the ship's creator Weir, checks out the Event Horizon, a spacecraft that ghosted years ago and just popped back up. Led by Capt. Miller, they start poking around the empty ship. But, dude, they quickly find out there's some freaky, sinister stuff lurking in there, and the nasty horrors from the ship's past are still hanging around. It's a deep-space nightmare!
