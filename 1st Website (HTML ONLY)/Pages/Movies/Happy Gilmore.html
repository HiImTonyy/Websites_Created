Movie Synopsis

In "Happy Gilmore," Happy's all about that hockey dream, but dude, turns out he's a natural at golf instead. When his grandma's about to lose her house, Happy hits the golf circuit to win some dough and save the day. With his wicked long drives and wild attitude, he shakes up the stuffy golf world, becoming an out-of-the-box hero on the greens. It's a hilarious, underdog story with a twist!
