Movie Synopsis

In "Home Alone," 8-year-old Kevin McCallister gets benched to the attic 'cause he's acting up pre-Paris vacay. Next thing, his fam jets off, forgetting him! Kevin's stoked at first, thinking his wish for no fam came true. But the party ends when he figures out two goofy burglars are eyeing his house. Now, it's up to Kevin to booby-trap the digs and save his turf. Total kid-versus-crooks showdown!
