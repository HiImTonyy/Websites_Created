Movie Synopsis

In "Army of Darkness," Ash gets zapped back to medieval times and nabbed by the tough Lord Arthur. Rockin' his trusty chainsaw, Ash is on a wild quest to snag the Book of the Dead, this wicked book that can call up a ghoul army. It's a killer mix of danger and rad chainsaw action!
