Movie Synopsis

In "Scream," some psycho in a Ghostface mask is offing teens in Woodsboro, California, playing by classic slasher flick rules. Sidney Prescott, still shook from her mom's murder, dodges the killer. Teaming up with goofy cop Dewey Riley and go-getter reporter Gale Weathers, Sidney's on a mission to reveal who's behind the mask. It's a total edge-of-your-seat, who-dunnit thrill ride!
