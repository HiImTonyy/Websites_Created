Movie Synopsis

In "Men in Black," Kay and Jay are these undercover agents in a top-secret government gig, managing Earth's alien visitors. They're checking out some sketchy alien activity when they stumble onto a gnarly plot by a space terrorist aiming to off two alien bigwigs chilling in NYC. It's all about cool gadgets, slick suits, and saving the world from an out-of-this-world threat!
