Movie Synopsis

In "Wayne's World," the rad "Saturday Night Live" skit hits the big screen. Rob Lowe's this slick producer trying to take Wayne and Garth's chill public access show to the big leagues of commercial TV. Wayne and Garth rock out to keep their show real and save Wayne's girlfriend from Lowe's smooth moves. It's a total head-banging, laugh-riot adventure, dude!
