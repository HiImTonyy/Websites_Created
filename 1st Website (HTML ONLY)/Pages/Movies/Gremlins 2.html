Movie Synopsis

In "Gremlins 2," after Gizmo's magic shop home gets wrecked, he winds up in this mega skyscraper. Billy Peltzer and his soon-to-be wife, Kate, who've tangled with Gremlins before, find out Gizmo's got a whole new crew of reptile-like rascals in the building. They're hustling to keep these critters from hitting the New York City streets, but this fresh batch of Gremlins is like a wild party that won't quit!
