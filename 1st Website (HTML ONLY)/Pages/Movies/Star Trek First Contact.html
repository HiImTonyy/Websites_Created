Movie Synopsis

In "Star Trek: First Contact," the Enterprise crew time-warps after a Borg ship to stop them from conquering old-school Earth. Stranded in the past, Geordi's helping out the dude inventing warp drive, while Captain Picard and Commander Data throw down against the Borg Queen who's trying to hijack the Enterprise. It's a space-time throwdown of epic proportions!
