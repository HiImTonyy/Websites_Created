Movie Synopsis

In "Demolition Man," tough cop John Spartan and wild thug Simon Phoenix get frozen in this CryoPrison. Fast forward 36 years, and it's 2032 in a peaceful L.A., now called San Angeles. But when Phoenix breaks loose, Spartan's thawed out to bring him down. Teaming up with future cop Lenina, it's all about catching this blast from the past in a world that's changed big time. Total action-packed time-trip, dude!
