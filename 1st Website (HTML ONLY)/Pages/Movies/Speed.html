Movie Synopsis

In "Speed," L.A. cop Jack ticks off this retired bomb dude, Howard Payne, by messing up his hostage gig. Payne hits back by rigging a bus with a bomb that blows if the bus dips under 50 mph. Jack, with help from cool passenger Annie and his partner Harry, races to save the bus crew and outsmart Payne, who's watching their every move. It's high-speed, high-stakes action, man!
