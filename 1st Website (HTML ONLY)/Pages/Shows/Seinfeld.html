Show Synopsis

"Seinfeld," with Jerry Seinfeld playing himself, is this epic show about a NYC stand-up comic. His life's a total circus, thanks to his oddball buddies. They're always diving into wild, sometimes petty, life puzzles. It's like a laugh-a-minute ride through everyday craziness!
