Show Synopsis

"The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air" is all about Will Smith playing a cooler-than-cool version of himself. He's this slick teen from West Philly sent to live with his rich uncle and aunt in Bel-Air. Will's street-smart ways totally clash with his fancy new fam, making for some hilarious, fresh-outta-Philly comedy!
